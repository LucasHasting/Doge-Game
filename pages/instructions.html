Doge Game Instructions by Lucas Hasting

The currency to purchase items is WOW

Click on the Doge to gain WOW




Barks: Increase the amount of WOW you get from clicking on Doge.


Super Barks: Increase the amount of WOW you get from clicking on Doge by alot.


Doge: Hire a Doge worker to gain more WOW over time.


Super Doge: Hire a Super Doge worker to gain even more WOW over time.
